Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Decleration of Independence include

Thomas Jefferson
The primary author of the Declaration, Jefferson is one of the most well-known figures associated with American independence.
John Adams
A passionate advocate for independence, Adams would later become the second President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned diplomat, inventor, and statesman, Franklin was one of the most influential figures in early American history.
Samuel Adams
A leader of the American Revolution, Samuel Adams was instrumental in organizing resistance against British rule, including the Boston Tea Party.
John Hancock
Known for his bold and prominent signature, Hancock was the president of the Continental Congress at the time of the Declaration's signing.
Richard Henry Lee
He famously proposed the resolution for independence to the Continental Congress, which led to the drafting of the Declaration.

These figures were central to the movement for American independence, and their signatures remain symbolic of the fight for freedom.
