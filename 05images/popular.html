More About the Top 3

2024 Top 3:

Cinnamon Roll
He was first introduced to the public in 2001, and goods/merch was released in 2002. He is a white puppy with long ears and a tail that looks like a cinnamon roll.
Pochaco
He was first introduced to the public in 1989, his birthday is Febuary 29 which makes him a leap year baby. Pochaco's name comes from the japanese word pocha pocha which means "chubby".
Kuromi
She was first introduced to the public in 2005 she first appeared in the TV series Onegai My Melody. She is a black cat with orange hair and devil wings, and is known for being mischievous & is My Melody's self proclaimed rival.
