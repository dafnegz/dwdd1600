Common Bees in the USA

Apidae
Ths is the biggest bee family with almost 6,000 different species containing honeybees, bumblebees, carpenter bees, and cuckoo bees (stinging bees belong to this family). While honey bees and bumblebees are social 90% of bees are solitary. This bee family usually consits of large and hairy bees.
Andrenidae
These bees are the mining bees, they're active earlier in the year and are known to be more solitary. They are usually ground nesters. These bees can come in many different sizes, shapes, and colors and can be selevtive of what plants they polinate.
Halictidae
These bees are commonly refferd to as Sweet bees they are the second largest family of bees. These bees are small between 1/4 and 3/4 inch in length. The colors of these bees can brown or black but there are also metalic variations that can be blue, green, or purple.
